# SAFETY
Rule 1 — Human oversight ensures that no AI output causes irreversible consequences without a person reviewing it. This is critical for anything touching users, money, or sensitive workflows.
Rule 2 — Minimal data access limits what your AI can "see." If it doesn't need a piece of data to do its job, it shouldn't have it. This protects privacy and shrinks your attack surface.
Rule 3 — Transparent failure means your AI should never pretend to know something it doesn't. Silent hallucinations or swallowed errors are far more dangerous than a clear "I'm not sure" message.
